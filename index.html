How to Play

Try to guess the target color by selecting one shade each of Red, Green, and Blue from the rows below.

  • The target color is shown at the top and stays visible throughout the game.
  • Each color row has 10 shades, from lightest (left) to darkest (right), packed tightly with no gaps.
  • Click one box per row to select that shade. Selected boxes get a black border.
